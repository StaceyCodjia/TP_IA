Shinra Tensei is a technique used through the Deva Path of the Rinnegan, said to be its pinnacle ability.&#91;1&#93;

Overview[]

Shinra Tensei can only be used by those who can manipulate the five basic nature transformations.&#91;1&#93; The user creates a "repulsive force" (斥力&#44; sekiryoku), which is generally used to push away everything in the vicinity.&#91;2&#93; Users can narrow the repulsion's direction, only pushing away things on certain sides so that nearby allies will be unaffected.&#91;3&#93; This repulsive force is most commonly created around the user as a defence, repelling nearby opponents or incoming ninjutsu;&#91;4&#93; it can also be used to disperse ninjutsu that have already struck the user, such as Amaterasu.&#91;5&#93; Alternatively, the repulsive force can be created away from the user, destroying whatever is at the designated location.&#91;6&#93;

Shinra Tensei's strength and area of effect are determined by how much chakra is used. In its typical usage, it repels only within a few feet's distance and has enough force to destroy earthen constructs.&#91;2&#93; Nagato, after cutting off control of his other Six Paths of Pain and channelling all his chakra to the Deva Path, performs a Shinra Tensei that is large enough and powerful enough to reduce Konohagakure to a deep crater; doing this shortens his life&#91;6&#93; and afterwards dulls the responsiveness of his Six Paths of Pain.&#91;7&#93; At its standard strength, opponents can resist Shinra Tensei by securing themselves to the ground: coating their feet with chakra to increase traction,&#91;8&#93; digging in deeply enough to counter repulsion,&#91;9&#93; or grouping together to minimise the repulsive force's influence.&#91;10&#93; Shinra Tensei also exerts a recoil on the user. If the opponent can withstand the repulsive force and force the user to use enough force, they can turn Shinra Tensei against the user.&#91;9&#93;

After Shinra Tensei is performed, there is a brief cooldown period during which it and other Deva Path abilities cannot be used. At a minimum, this cooldown is five seconds. During large-scale uses, such as the one that destroyed Konoha, the cooldown takes several minutes.&#91;11&#93;

Influence[]

The names of Shinra Tensei and its companion ability, Banshō Ten'in, are derived from Shinra Banshō (神羅万象&#44; literally meaning: Everything Covered by God), a Japanese four-character idiom and homophone variant of Shinra Banshō (森羅万象&#44; literally meaning: All-Covering Forests and Ten Thousand Things), a Buddhist idiom which has come to mean "All of Nature" or "All of Creation".

Trivia[]

 	 	 	 		 			 		 		 		 			The Shinra Tensei orb.
 		 	 
In the anime, Shinra Tensei is sometimes depicted as an orb of varying colours which surrounds the Deva Path.

In Naruto Shippūden: Shinobi Rumble, an enhanced, "Full Throttle" version of the technique called "Shinra Tensei: Seiatsu" (神羅天征・征圧&#44; literally meaning: Heavenly Subjugation of the Omnipresent God: Subjugating Pressure) can be performed.

In Naruto Shippūden: Ultimate Ninja Storm 4, Sasuke can use Shinra Tensei while he is in his Complete Body — Susanoo form.

In one instance, Obito Uchiha using his own Dōjutsu's ability to summon large-scale weapons from his Kamui dimension,&#91;12&#93; was, in the anime, accompanied by the sound effect affiliated with Shinra Tensei's repulsive force, indicating that he instantly propelled the projectiles towards his opponent, significantly increasing the attack's destructive range and impact.&#91;13&#93;

In the manga, Kaguya attacks Naruto and his clones with a repulsive force by extending her hand, but the name of the jutsu used is not mentioned.&#91;14&#93; In the Jump Force game, where the ability is seen again, it is confirmed to be Shinra Tensei.

In the anime, Momoshiki repels a burst of Fūma Shuriken through a jutsu with repulsive capabilities, but does not mention its name. Even so, the jutsu in question has the same characteristics as Shinra Tensei, is used through the Rinnegan and Momoshiki has demonstrated that he meets the the above-mentioned, necessary requirements.&#91;15&#93;
In Naruto to Boruto: Shinobi Striker, Momoshiki (who is available as DLC), by stretching out both of his hands (each to the left and the right) and after landing a few hits with a weak attack in close combat, can generate a shockwave with his red palm eyes. It not only knocks back all enemies within range but also damages and/or destroys them. The player can unlock the palm eyes as "weapons" by training under him as his student. Unlike Nagato and Sasuke (in the games), however, the shockwave generated by Momoshiki's palm eyes with Shinra Tensei has no white shimmer, but a black one.
